# Security Policy

## Reporting a Vulnerability

At RADCO, we take security seriously. If you discover a security vulnerability within Rady AI or any of our services, we appreciate your help in disclosing it to us responsibly.

**Please email all reports to:** `report@radyai.com`

We aim to acknowledge receipt of your vulnerability report within 48 hours and will keep you informed of our progress throughout the resolution process.

---

## Bug Bounty Program

We believe in rewarding researchers who help us keep Rady AI secure. 

### Rewards
For each **valid, previously unreported security vulnerability** that you find and report, you will receive:

🎁 **3 months of Rady Premium for free**

### Eligibility
- The issue must be a genuine security vulnerability (not a feature request, configuration issue, or already known bug)
- You must be the first to report the specific issue
- The report must include clear steps to reproduce
- You must not exploit the vulnerability beyond what's necessary for demonstration
- You must not access, modify, or delete other users' data

### Scope
The program covers:
- The main Rady AI platform at https://radyai.com
- All subdomains listed in our tools
- API endpoints
- Authentication mechanisms

### Out of Scope
- Social engineering attacks
- Denial of service attacks
- Physical attempts against property or data centers
- Issues related to browser extensions or third-party software

---

## Disclosure Policy

- Report vulnerabilities privately via `report@radyai.com`
- Allow us reasonable time to investigate and address the issue before any public disclosure
- Make every effort to avoid privacy violations, data destruction, and service disruption

We're committed to working with the security community to verify, reproduce, and resolve vulnerabilities quickly. Thank you for helping keep Rady AI and our users safe.
